Computer Learning

Students are invited to take part in two research projects.Both projects use BlackRose technology as a server and database platform. Possible integration with Sun ONE family of servers .Sun ONE Studio will be a recommended development tool. As a OS platform Linux, Solaris, Windows2000 can be chosen. BlackRose was developed by Andrzej Popielewicz. It is written in Java and is www and CORBA oriented.

Projects in preparation

Projects are planned to start in Spring Semester February 2003. Participation of students still requires acceptance of authorities of the Physics department.
  • StudentServer 
    • StudentServer is a server helping to manage exercises for the students.Its main function is to supply the students with information concerning specific exercises.
  • StudentAdviserServer 
    • This project can be assigned to new emerging e-learning discipline. It should help to teach students remotely and test their progress in learning. Artificial Intelligence methods with speech recognition etc will be involved.

Requirements

  • Finished 3 years of studies with good average quote.
  • Good working knowledge of Java (test)
  • Good working knowledge of English (test)
  • Applications created within this project will be the property of A.Mickiewicz University
